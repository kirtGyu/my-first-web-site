BricQ

An engaging introductory hands-on STEAM solution encourages students to discover physical science in action. LEGO® Education BricQ Motion engages elementary and middle-school students in STEAM learning as they experiment with forces, motion and interactions in the context of sports. BricQ Motion provides easy, hands-on learning experiences that spark cool “aha” moments, as students set LEGO bricks in motion without the need for technology.
